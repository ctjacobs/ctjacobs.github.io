A paper which describes the Introduction to Programming for Geoscientists course throughout the years 2010 to 2014 has been accepted for publication in the Journal of Geoscience Education:

  • C. T. Jacobs, G. J. Gorman, H. E. Rees, L. Craig (To Appear). Experiences with efficient methodologies for teaching computer programming to geoscientists. Journal of Geoscience Education. Pre-print: http://arxiv.org/abs/1505.05425
The effectiveness of the teaching approach and methodology as it changed over these 5 years is discussed and evaluated with respect to final exam marks, formal course and lecturer evaluations, and informal formative student comments.
