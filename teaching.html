A paper which describes the Introduction to Programming for Geoscientists course has been published in the Journal of Geoscience Education. The effectiveness of the teaching approach and methodology as it changed over 5 years is discussed and evaluated with respect to final exam marks, formal course and lecturer evaluations, and informal formative student comments:

  • C. T. Jacobs, G. J. Gorman, H. E. Rees, L. E. Craig (2016). Experiences With Efficient Methodologies for Teaching Computer Programming to Geoscientists. Journal of Geoscience Education, 64(3):183-198. DOI: 10.5408/15-101.1. Pre-print: http://arxiv.org/abs/1505.05425
